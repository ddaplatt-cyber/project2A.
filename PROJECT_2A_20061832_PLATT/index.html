The Plan

STATEMENT

A portfolio page that is simple to navigate and visually appealing with a minimalistic yet expressive design. Through the intent to further my film career, this website will provide the bridge between my work and potential collaborators, customers and investors in projects. This website will convey the following:

  • Who I am
  • What I do
  • How I do it
  • Why I do it
  • Where to reach me
I enjoy simple designs when it comes to websites and find that the more convoluted the home page, the more difficult it is to navigate. A straightforward approach will be taken to avoid a distracting or disorderly site. To increase viewer engagement, I aim to include in-site videos and star ratings. Carousels and stationary scrolling segments for photo display features will also be used. The subsequent pages will provide the plans for said page.

ANALYSIS

Fisk Projects

The use of eye-catching graphics coupled with bold, bright colours is hard to ignore. Yet for me, it is quite distracting to have so many things going on at once. The confronting logo is something that I will be taking to my personal page, as well as the grid structure for the content. Having each project easily identifiable, labelled and sublabeled in a readable and authentic way was very interesting to see and may be implemented. The auto play of photos is also a feature I will try to implement.

Best Aspects

  1. Tone and Appeal
  2. Graphic Design
  3. Bright bold colour scheme
Sebastian Martinez

The muted tones and plain white background contrasted with the rich imagery were very enjoyable in this site. The interactive component was a very welcome surprise, yet I found it to also be distracting and overcompensating for the lack of text and content. The animated text was inspiring, as was the typography and hierarchy. With such variation in text, you were immediately drawn to the most important information first. The minimal approach to displaying his skills and products in a uniquely creative and authentic way was very intriguing.

Best Aspects

  1. Interactivity
  2. minimal colour scheme
  3. Typography
